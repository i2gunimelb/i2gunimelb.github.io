About

I2G is a GSA affiliated grad group started in 2016 by the graduate research students of University of Melbourne, who completed their Masters from IITs and IISc [IIT- Indian Institute of Technology; IISc- Indian Institute of Science], under the guidance of MIPP staff. The group’s primary aims are to enhance the academic and professional life of its members, to enhance social interaction and promote the welfare of fellow graduate students and to act as a university-wide representative group of research graduate students from IITs and IISc. This year, the group opened its membership to all Indian graduate students and their families.

We believe in radiating the goodness of Indian culture across all Melbourne university graduate students. Hence, all our events are free and members are encouraged to bring along their friends to most of our events. Another way we reach out to more people is by organizing mutually beneficial collaborative events with various groups and organizations. This year we had collaborated/joint events with organizations like Melbourne India Post Graduate Program (MIPP), Australia India Institute (AII), IIT Alumni Victoria (IITaV) and UniMelb groups like Graduate Backyard Cricket Club (GBCC), Graduate Power Shutlers (GPS) and Indian Graduate Students Society (IGSS).

Events

Festivals are an integral part of India and we can never have enough of them! Each year, the group celebrates the following festivals - Diwali (the Indian festival of lights in end-October/November), Christmas/New Year (in December) and Holi (the Indian Festival of Colors in March) and members along with their friends and relatives are welcome. This way the group brings the vibrancy and exuberance of Indian culture to life in Melbourne and lets its members and their friends experience the richness of Indian traditions miles away from home.
